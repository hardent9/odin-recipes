Lasagna for Odin

Description

A delicious an easy to make layered casserole dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced (approx. 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, mixed
  • 1 28-ounce can tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste (1/2 a 6-ounce can)
  • 1 14 ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano (or 2 teaspoons dried)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • Pinch garlic powder or garlic salt, to taste
  • About 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar, to taste
  • Sugar to taste, not more than 1/4 cup
  • Salt to taste
